ZSIDEO Content LLC

1. What is ZSIDEO Content and what services do you offer?

ZSIDEO Content is a media and content agency helping creators and businesses grow. We provide short-form video editing, content strategy, brand storytelling, and full content system builds.

2. How does ZSIDEO ensure that videos are human-edited and high quality?

All videos are first edited by our human editors, then reviewed by our QC team to check B-roll, captions, motion graphics, and brand consistency.

3. What platforms does ZSIDEO specialize in?

We focus on TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and long-form YouTube content, optimizing each video for its platform’s algorithm.

4. How long does onboarding and first video delivery take?

Onboarding typically takes 24–48 hours. The first video is delivered according to the agreed schedule, usually within 1–2 days after onboarding.

5. How many videos can I realistically expect per month or per week?

The number of videos depends on the package purchased. For example, short-form packages may deliver 1 video per day. Our workflow ensures consistent daily delivery.

6. What happens if I need revisions or changes after delivery?

Clients can request revisions anytime. Revisions are handled promptly, and our dedicated editor ensures updates are implemented efficiently.

7. Do you offer refunds or revisions if deliverables don’t meet expectations?

Yes. If the agreed deliverables are not met after a fair revision, clients can request a refund or credit according to our transparent policy.

8. How does ZSIDEO communicate and update clients during editing and delivery?

Our CSM team provides regular updates on Slack or email. Clients are informed about progress, revisions, and schedules to ensure smooth collaboration.

9. How can I be sure that ZSIDEO is legitimate and not a scam?

ZSIDEO has worked with hundreds of verified clients. All past workflow issues were operational, not scams. Our current workflow is transparent and accountable.

10. Can I see examples of past work, client testimonials or case studies?

Yes. We maintain a public portfolio with real client videos, case studies, and testimonials on our website and social media channels.

11. What editing software and tools does ZSIDEO use?

We use DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop, and selected AI tools for efficiency, while ensuring human quality control.

12. How do you handle data security, raw footage confidentiality, and client privacy?

All client files are stored securely in Google Drive with restricted access. We respect privacy and follow strict internal protocols for data protection.
